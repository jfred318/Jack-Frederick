With a strong passion for business/data analytics, it has been exciting for me to explore ways to expand my knowledge both in and out of the classroom.

For an assignment in my class “The Power of AI: Fundamentals to Applications,” I analyzed energy consumption trends in a large data set that featured variables like temperature, occupancy, and humidity in various buildings. This reinforced my passion for analytics as I began to learn how closely tied data collection and AI efficiency are. I was able to use my experience in a high school class, AP Stats, to aid me in my assessment of the data. Among the images below, is a copy of this assignment.
